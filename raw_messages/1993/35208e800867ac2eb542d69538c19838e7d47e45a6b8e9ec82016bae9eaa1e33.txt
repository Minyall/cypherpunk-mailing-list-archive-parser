Just to remind everyone, the clipper key is not 40 bits.
 
It is 80 bits.
 
It is not divided up into two 40-bit halves.
 
It is the result of xor'ing two 80-bit numbers, each of which would be held
separately.  Knowing one of the numbers would not improve your chances of
guessing the key.
