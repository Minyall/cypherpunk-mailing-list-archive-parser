Here is some info on Texas ids:

Polaroid photo, blue background, right side of the id.
Plastic laminate, green state seal on photo and printed part.
Plastic laminate also embossed/watermarked with state seal (visible at an angle)
Info on license:
	Class (type of vehicle)
	TX drivers number (not SSN) 8 digits
	Endorsement (more on type of vehicle)
	Restrictions, for example A=vision
	height
	sex
	organ donor yes or no
	birthdate
	expiration year (on birthdate), good for 4 years
	DPS audit number, 11 digits

	Last, First Middle 
	Address
	signature
